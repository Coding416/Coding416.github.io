Shawarma Ground Beef Rice Bowls

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes

Total Time: 40 minutes Yields: 4

These garlic feta shawarma beef bowls are one of those meals that hit every craving at once. The ground beef is deeply spiced and saucy, the garlic feta dressing is creamy and tangy, and everything comes together perfectly over warm yellow rice. Simple ingredients, big flavor, and exactly the kind of bowl I love making on repeat.
